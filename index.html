About the Company

Mediconnect is a healthcare technology company specializing in providing innovative solutions to improve patient care. Our mission is to revolutionize the healthcare industry through cutting-edge technology and a commitment to excellence.

Founded in 2010, Mediconnect has since established itself as a leader in the field, working closely with healthcare providers to streamline processes, enhance efficiency, and ultimately deliver better outcomes for patients.

Our team consists of talented individuals from diverse backgrounds, all united by a passion for using technology to make a positive impact on people's lives. Whether it's developing groundbreaking software or implementing innovative systems, we are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in healthcare.
